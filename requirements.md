Users

      - Functional Requirements
          - Make Updates to articles
          - Log in Credentials
          - Make comments on articles
          - Make recommendations for photos

       - Functional Requirements
          - Upload photos to databases

          - Submit articles to Editor
          - Log in credentials
          - Respond to comments from Editor with changes
